Been getting alot of DMs so i will clarify here when i type something on this chat
HOD- High of the day
LOD- low of the day
3
The scalping strategy i use trades only HOD breaks and LOD breaks. ALSO Pre-maket LOD and HOD breaks.
When i say "ticker name" plus a break level. I DONT mean ENTER NOW. It means i am watching that level and if i like the break, i will take the trade. IT DOESNT MEAN ENTER NOW.
For Example:
"BABA 310 break"- Means i am watching BABA to hopefully break 310 and i will enter for a scalp.
It also means i am watching that level. So enter the trade if the break occurs based on your own risk tolerance and management.
